Our Services

Buying & Selling Accountancy Practices

I'm Looking to Sell

Deciding when and how to exit your practice is one of the most significant financial decisions of your career. We guide you through every step — ensuring you achieve maximum value while protecting your clients, your staff and your legacy.

We work on a success-only basis, meaning there are no upfront fees. We only earn when your deal completes.

  • Confidential practice valuation
  • Access to our network of qualified buyers
  • Buyer assessment — cultural fit, not just headline price
  • Deal structuring: earn-outs, deferred consideration & more
  • Support from heads of terms through to completion
  • Network of legal, compliance and tax specialists

About Oakfront Capital

The Trusted Name in Accountancy M&A

Oakfront Capital is a leading intermediary in the sale and acquisition of UK accountancy practices. Our team brings decades of combined sector experience and an unrivalled network of buyers — from sole trader acquirers to national consolidators.

We understand that the headline value of a sale is not always the most important factor. Cultural fit, client continuity, staff welfare and earn-out terms can matter just as much — and we help you navigate all of it.

The cost of our associated compliance, legal and tax support network is covered by the buyer. Our fee is success-only: we are only paid when your transaction completes.
